# Privacy Policy for ModeMap

**Effective Date:** July 16, 2026  
**Developer:** denodes  
**Contact:** iliadenis.de@gmail.com

## Overview

ModeMap is a room acoustic analysis application designed to calculate and visualize acoustic room modes for rectangular and arbitrary-shaped rooms. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection

**ModeMap does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or user information.** All calculations and analyses are performed entirely on your device (on-device only).

### What We Don't Collect
- Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
- Location data
- Device identifiers or unique identifiers
- Browsing or usage history
- Audio recordings or acoustic samples
- Any calculation or analysis data

### Room Parameters and Calculations

All room dimensions, acoustic parameters, and mode calculations are processed locally on your device. This data is never sent to any server and is only retained in your device's local storage as long as you keep it in the app.

## Third-Party Services

ModeMap contains **no advertising, no analytics, and no third-party SDKs of any kind.** It does not connect to the internet at all — every calculation runs entirely on your device, and the app has no server to talk to.

## Purchases

ModeMap is a one-time paid app, purchased through Google Play. Payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play's billing system — ModeMap itself never sees or stores your payment information. See [Google Play's privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for how Google handles purchase data.

## Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. All your acoustic analysis data remains under your sole control on your device.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted through app updates. Your continued use of ModeMap following any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated privacy policy.
